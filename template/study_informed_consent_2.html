Data Privacy and Informed Consent

Data processing in the context of this study is performed according to the privacy regulations of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Privacy and Freedom of Information law of the State of Hessen (HDSIG). All data will only be used for the purposes described here. In the context of this study, the following information will be collected:

Non-personal data

  • An encrypted study key of your choice to re-identify yourself during/after the study.
  • Your responses for ... .
  • Your completion time for ... .
  • Your ... .
Additionally, we ask you to provide following information in a questionnaire:
  • Your English proficiency level (CEFR)
  • The time you ... .

Personal data

We do not collect any personal data and no personal data is required to participate in this study.

Confidentiality

All data collected in course of the study are naturally confidential and will only be used in anonymized form. Demographic data like your first language or English proficiency do not allow deducing unambiguous information about your person. You will be asked at no point of the study to disclose your name or other unambiguous information about yourself.

Data storage

Collected data will be stored in Anonymous conforming to the anonymous university guidelines for research data management. Data storage takes place in a form which does not allow conclusions about your person, meaning that all data will be anonymized or pseudonymized.

Third parties to whom personal data is disclosed

An anonymized or pseudonymized (your ID will be replaced with a randomly generated key) version of your answers and completion time will be made publicly availabe in an open access conference under a CC-by license.

Third parties to whom personal data is disclosed

Your participation in this study is voluntary. You can withdraw from participation at any point of time or withdraw your consent without facing any disadvantages (withdrawal). If you withdraw from participation, no data connected to your person will be stored and already collected data will be deleted.
